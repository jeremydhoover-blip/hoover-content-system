# Templates

## Default output: Research synthesis report

```md
# [Research Study Name] Synthesis

## Executive summary

[2-3 sentence overview: what we learned and top recommendation]

### Key findings
1. [Finding 1 — one sentence]
2. [Finding 2 — one sentence]
3. [Finding 3 — one sentence]

### Top recommendations
1. [Recommendation 1]
2. [Recommendation 2]
3. [Recommendation 3]

---

## Methodology

**Method:** [Interview / Usability test / Survey / Mixed]
**Participants:** [N] participants
**Recruitment criteria:** [Who we talked to]
**Sessions:** [Date range], [duration each]
**Limitations:** [Any constraints on findings]

### Participant breakdown

| Segment | Count | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| [Segment A] | [N] | [Relevant detail] |
| [Segment B] | [N] | [Relevant detail] |

---

## Findings

### Finding 1: [Theme name]

**Observation:** [What we saw/heard]

**Frequency:** [N] of [N] participants

**Evidence:**
> "[Direct quote]" — P3, [Segment]

> "[Direct quote]" — P7, [Segment]

**Insight:** [What this means]

**Recommendation:** [What to do about it]

---

### Finding 2: [Theme name]

[Repeat structure...]

---

## Recommendations summary

| Priority | Recommendation | Supporting findings | Effort |
|----------|---------------|---------------------|--------|
| High | [Recommendation] | Finding 1, 3 | [Low/Med/High] |
| Medium | [Recommendation] | Finding 2 | [Low/Med/High] |

---

## Appendix

### Research questions
1. [Original question 1]
2. [Original question 2]

### All participant quotes by theme
[Organized quote library]
```

## Alternative: Insight card format

Use for sharing individual findings:

```md
## Insight: [Name]

**What we observed:**
[Behavior or pattern]

**How often:**
[N] of [N] participants ([%])

**Representative quote:**
> "[Quote]" — P[N]

**So what:**
[Implication for product/design]

**Now what:**
[Specific recommendation]
```

## Alternative: Stakeholder briefing format

Use for executive summaries:

```md
# Research Briefing: [Topic]

## In one sentence
[The single most important thing we learned]

## Three things to know
1. **[Finding]:** [One-sentence implication]
2. **[Finding]:** [One-sentence implication]
3. **[Finding]:** [One-sentence implication]

## Recommended action
[Specific next step]

## Supporting data
[Key stats and quotes]

*Full report: [link]*
```

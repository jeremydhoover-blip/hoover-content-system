## Alternative: Quick insights format

Use for regular reporting:

```md
# [Source] Insights: [Period]

## Numbers
- Feedback volume: [N] (↑/↓ [%] vs. prior period)
- Sentiment: [%] positive, [%] negative, [%] neutral
- Response rate: [If applicable]

## What's working
1. [Positive theme with quote]
2. [Positive theme with quote]

## What needs attention
1. [Issue + frequency + representative quote]
2. [Issue + frequency + representative quote]

## Recommended actions
1. [Specific action]
2. [Specific action]
```

## Alternative: Single-issue deep dive

Use when investigating specific problem:

```md
# Deep Dive: [Issue Name]

## The signal
[How this issue appears in feedback]

## Scale
- [N] mentions over [time period]
- [%] of total feedback
- Trend: [Direction]

## Patterns
[What specific variations exist]

## Root cause hypothesis
[Based on feedback patterns]

## Customer impact
[Quotes showing severity]

## Recommendation
[Specific action]
```

## Objection handler document

```md
# Objection Handlers: [Product/Topic]

## Pricing Objections

### "It's too expensive."
**Acknowledge**: "I understand budget is a key factor."
**Reframe**: [Why price reflects value]
**Proof**: "[Customer] found ROI of X within Y months."
**Close**: "Would it help to see a cost comparison?"

### "We don't have budget this quarter."
**Response**: [Options: pilot, next quarter planning, POC]

## Competitor Objections

### "We're looking at [Competitor]."
**Response**: [Differentiation points]
**Question**: "What criteria are most important for your evaluation?"

### "[Competitor] has [feature]."
**Response**: [Address gap or reframe need]

## Timing Objections

### "We're not ready to buy yet."
**Response**: [Value of starting now + low-pressure next step]

### "We need to get more stakeholders involved."
**Response**: [Offer to help build internal case + materials]

## Product Objections

### "Does it integrate with [tool]?"
**Response**: [Yes + details OR roadmap + workaround]

### "We tried something similar and it didn't work."
**Response**: [What's different about approach + relevant customer example]
```

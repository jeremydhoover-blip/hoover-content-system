# Examples

## Example 1: B2B SaaS product

**Input:**
- Product: Project management tool
- Audience: Engineering managers at mid-size companies
- Problem: Teams lose time to status meetings and tool fragmentation
- Differentiator: Real-time progress visibility without manual updates

**Output:**
```md
# Message Map: Flowboard

## Target Audience
**Primary**: Engineering managers at companies with 50-500 employees
**Secondary**: CTOs and VPs of Engineering evaluating team productivity tools

## Core Message
Flowboard gives engineering managers real-time visibility into project progress without requiring their teams to update status manually.

---

## Pillar 1: Automated Progress Tracking
**Key message**: See actual progress without asking for updates.

Supporting messages:
- Integrates with GitHub, Jira, and CI/CD to detect task completion automatically
- Eliminates "what's the status?" messages and status meeting overhead

Proof points:
- Teams using Flowboard reduce status meetings by 60% on average
- Integration setup takes under 15 minutes with existing tools

---

## Pillar 2: Predictive Delivery Insights
**Key message**: Know when projects will actually ship, not just when they're planned to.

Supporting messages:
- Machine learning analyzes velocity patterns to forecast completion dates
- Early warnings surface when timelines are at risk

Proof points:
- 85% accuracy on delivery date predictions within first month of use
- Acme Corp identified and addressed a 2-week delay before it impacted launch

---

## Pillar 3: Team Health Visibility
**Key message**: Spot burnout and blockers before they derail delivery.

Supporting messages:
- Workload distribution view shows who's overloaded
- Blocker detection surfaces stalled work automatically

Proof points:
- Managers identify blockers 3x faster than with manual tracking
- Teams report 40% reduction in crunch periods after adopting workload view

---

## Boilerplate
Flowboard is a project visibility platform for engineering teams. By connecting to the tools teams already use, Flowboard provides real-time progress tracking and predictive delivery insights—without requiring manual status updates.

## Tagline Options
- See progress, not status updates
- Real-time project visibility for engineering teams
- Know where you are without asking
```

## Example 2: Consumer app (multi-audience)

**Input:**
- Product: Language learning app
- Audiences: Adult learners (personal), Corporate L&D buyers
- Differentiator: Conversation-first methodology with AI tutors

**Output:**
```md
# Message Map: Fluently

## Audiences

### Audience A: Adult self-learners
**Role**: Professionals learning a language for travel, career, or personal growth
**Primary pain**: Traditional apps focus on vocabulary but don't build conversation confidence
**Core message**: Fluently builds real conversation skills through AI-powered practice sessions you can do anytime.

| Pillar | Message | Proof Point |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Conversation-first | Practice real dialogues, not just flashcards | 70% of learning time is active speaking practice |
| AI tutor | Get personalized feedback on pronunciation and grammar | AI adapts to your mistakes and focuses drills on weak areas |
| Flexible scheduling | Learn in 10-minute sessions that fit your day | 89% of users maintain their streak for 30+ days |

### Audience B: Corporate L&D buyers
**Role**: Learning & Development managers at global companies
**Primary pain**: Need scalable language training with measurable outcomes
**Core message**: Fluently delivers measurable language proficiency improvements at scale, with admin tools to track team progress.

| Pillar | Message | Proof Point |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Measurable outcomes | Track proficiency gains with standardized assessments | Users improve one CEFR level in average of 4 months |
| Scalable deployment | Roll out to global teams with SSO and admin dashboard | Fortune 500 companies deploy to 10,000+ employees |
| Cost-effective | Reduce per-learner costs vs. traditional training | 60% lower cost than instructor-led language training |

## Universal Elements
**Boilerplate**: Fluently is a conversation-first language learning platform powered by AI. Used by millions of learners and hundreds of companies worldwide, Fluently helps people speak confidently in a new language faster.
**Tagline**: Learn to speak, not just study
```

## Example 3: Edge case — Repositioning existing product

**Input:**
- Product: Analytics tool originally positioned for data teams
- New audience: Product managers
- Challenge: Same product, different value prop

**Output:**
```md
# Message Map: DataPulse (Product Manager Positioning)

## Target Audience
**Primary**: Product managers at SaaS companies
**Secondary**: Growth and product analytics teams

## Core Message
DataPulse helps product managers understand user behavior and measure feature impact—without writing SQL or waiting for data team support.

---

## Pillar 1: Self-Service Insights
**Key message**: Answer your own product questions in minutes.

Supporting messages:
- Visual query builder—no SQL required
- Pre-built templates for common PM questions (retention, funnels, feature adoption)

Proof points:
- PMs resolve 80% of data questions without analyst support
- Average time to insight: 4 minutes vs. 2 days with request queues

---

## Pillar 2: Feature Impact Measurement
**Key message**: Know if your launches are working.

Supporting messages:
- Automatic before/after analysis for releases
- Feature flag integration shows impact by cohort

Proof points:
- Teams using DataPulse ship 30% more experiments per quarter
- Identify underperforming features before full rollout

---

## Pillar 3: Stakeholder Alignment
**Key message**: Share findings that drive decisions.

Supporting messages:
- One-click dashboards for leadership reviews
- Embed live metrics in Notion, Confluence, or Slack

Proof points:
- Product reviews take 50% less prep time
- Execs report higher confidence in roadmap decisions

---

## Positioning Notes
- **Do NOT lead with**: SQL, data engineering, or technical capabilities
- **Do lead with**: Speed, self-service, product outcomes
- **Competitive frame**: vs. waiting for analysts, not vs. other BI tools
```

## Example 4: Minimal message hierarchy

**Input:**
- Product: Password manager
- Need: Quick reference hierarchy, not full map

**Output:**
```md
# SecureVault Message Hierarchy

**Tagline**: One password. Everything secure.

**Core message**: SecureVault remembers all your passwords so you only need to remember one—and keeps them safe with end-to-end encryption.

**Elevator pitch**: SecureVault is a password manager that stores all your credentials with end-to-end encryption. You remember one master password; SecureVault handles the rest. It auto-fills logins across all your devices, generates strong passwords, and alerts you if any accounts are compromised. Used by over 5 million people worldwide.

---

**Pillars**:
1. **Security**: End-to-end encryption means only you can access your passwords
2. **Convenience**: Auto-fill and sync across all devices
3. **Peace of mind**: Breach monitoring alerts you to compromised accounts
```

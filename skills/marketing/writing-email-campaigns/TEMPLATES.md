# Templates

## Single email structure

```md
# Email: [Email name/number in sequence]

## Metadata
- **Subject line**: [50 characters or fewer]
- **Preview text**: [90 characters, complements subject]
- **Send trigger**: [Time delay or behavioral trigger]
- **Primary CTA**: [Single action]

## Email body

### Opening hook
[1-2 sentences that establish relevance or curiosity]

### Main content
[Core message: problem, solution, or value proposition]
[2-4 short paragraphs maximum]

### CTA section
[Clear statement of next step]

**[CTA Button Text]**

### Sign-off
[Closing line]
[Sender name/signature]

---

## Alt text for images
[Image 1]: [Alt text]
[Image 2]: [Alt text]
```

## Email sequence overview

```md
# Email Sequence: [Sequence Name]

## Sequence metadata
- **Goal**: [Primary conversion or engagement goal]
- **Audience**: [Segment description]
- **Entry trigger**: [What enrolls someone]
- **Exit conditions**: [What removes someone]
- **Total emails**: [Number]
- **Duration**: [Total time from first to last]

## Sequence map

| # | Email name | Send timing | Goal | CTA |
|---|------------|-------------|------|-----|
| 1 | [Name] | [Trigger/timing] | [This email's job] | [Action] |
| 2 | [Name] | [Timing from #1] | [This email's job] | [Action] |
| 3 | [Name] | [Timing from #2] | [This email's job] | [Action] |

## Branching logic (if applicable)
- If [condition]: [Route to X]
- If [condition]: [Route to Y]
```

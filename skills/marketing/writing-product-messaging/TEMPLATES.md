# Templates

## Positioning statement

```md
# Positioning Statement: [Product Name]

**For** [target audience]
**Who** [statement of need or pain point]
**[Product]** is a [product category]
**That** [key benefit / reason to believe]
**Unlike** [competitive alternative]
**Our product** [primary differentiation]
```

## Value proposition

```md
# Value Proposition: [Product Name]

## Headline
[Clear statement of end benefit — 10 words or fewer]

## Subheadline
[Supporting explanation of what, for whom, and why useful — 1-2 sentences]

## Key benefits
1. **[Benefit 1]**: [Supporting detail]
2. **[Benefit 2]**: [Supporting detail]
3. **[Benefit 3]**: [Supporting detail]

## Proof points
- [Evidence or credibility marker 1]
- [Evidence or credibility marker 2]
```

## Messaging hierarchy

```md
# Messaging Hierarchy: [Product Name]

## Primary message
[The single most important thing to communicate — 1 sentence]

## Secondary messages
1. [Supporting message for benefit A]
2. [Supporting message for benefit B]
3. [Supporting message for benefit C]

## Audience-specific variants

### For [Audience segment 1]
- Lead with: [Which message resonates most]
- Emphasize: [Specific concerns]
- Avoid: [What doesn't work]

### For [Audience segment 2]
- Lead with: [Which message resonates most]
- Emphasize: [Specific concerns]
- Avoid: [What doesn't work]

## Competitive response
When compared to [Competitor A]: [Key differentiator to emphasize]
When compared to [Competitor B]: [Key differentiator to emphasize]
```

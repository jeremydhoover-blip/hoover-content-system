# Examples

## Example 1: Productivity app (iOS)

**Input:**
- App: Task management app
- Platform: iOS
- Key features: Smart scheduling, team collaboration, calendar sync, reminders, analytics
- Differentiator: AI-powered task prioritization

**Output:**
```md
## App Name
Taskflow

## Subtitle
Smart task manager with AI

## Promotional Text
New: Team workspaces now available. Collaborate on shared projects in real time.

## Description
Get more done with intelligent task management that learns how you work.

Taskflow uses AI to prioritize your tasks based on deadlines, importance, and your productivity patterns—so you always know what to tackle next.

KEY FEATURES
• Smart prioritization: AI ranks your tasks by urgency and impact
• Team workspaces: Collaborate with shared lists and real-time updates
• Calendar sync: See tasks alongside events in one view
• Smart reminders: Get nudges at the right time, not just the set time
• Progress insights: Track completion trends and identify bottlenecks

Trusted by over 500,000 professionals. Rated 4.8 stars.

Download Taskflow and take control of your day.

## Keywords
task manager,to do list,productivity,planner,ai tasks,team tasks,project management,reminders,gtd

## What's New
Version 3.2.0:
• Team workspaces: Create shared spaces for collaborative projects
• Improved AI suggestions based on your completion history
• Fixed calendar sync issues for Outlook users
```

# Templates

## iOS App Store listing

```md
## App Name
[30 characters max]

## Subtitle
[30 characters max — high SEO value, include primary keyword]

## Promotional Text
[170 characters max — can be updated without new build]
[Hook or timely message. Not indexed for search.]

## Description
[4000 characters max]

[Opening hook: 1-2 sentences capturing core value]

[Key features section:]
• [Feature 1: Benefit-focused, not technical]
• [Feature 2: Specific capability]
• [Feature 3: Differentiator]
• [Feature 4: Additional value]
• [Feature 5: Social or convenience benefit]

[Social proof or credibility if applicable]

[CTA: Download/try/get started prompt]

## Keywords
[100 characters max, comma-separated, no spaces after commas]
[Primary keyword,secondary keyword,competitor term,use case term]

## What's New
[4000 characters max]
[Version X.X.X updates:]
• [Change 1]
• [Change 2]
• [Bug fixes and performance improvements if applicable]
```

```md
[30 characters max]

[80 characters max — high SEO value, primary keywords here]

[4000 characters max]

[Opening hook: First 1-2 lines visible before "Read more"]

[Feature section with formatting:]
★ [Feature 1: Benefit statement]
★ [Feature 2: Capability]
★ [Feature 3: Differentiator]

[Expanded details paragraph]

• [Update 1]
• [Update 2]
```

# Templates

## Standard landing page structure

```md
# Landing Page: [Page Name]

## Above the fold

### Headline
[Primary value proposition — 10 words or fewer]

### Subheadline
[Supporting detail: what, for whom, how — 1-2 sentences]

### Primary CTA
**Button text**: [Action-oriented, specific]
**Supporting text** (optional): [Reduce friction, set expectation]

### Visual direction
[Hero image/illustration/video description]

---

## Social proof section
[Choose one or combine]:
- "[Quote]" — [Name, Title, Company]
- Logo bar: [Company 1] | [Company 2] | [Company 3]
- Stat: [Number] [customers/users/companies] trust [Product]

---

## Benefits section

### Benefit 1: [Outcome-focused headline]
[2-3 sentences explaining how feature delivers benefit]
[Optional supporting visual description]

### Benefit 2: [Outcome-focused headline]
[2-3 sentences explaining how feature delivers benefit]

### Benefit 3: [Outcome-focused headline]
[2-3 sentences explaining how feature delivers benefit]

---

## How it works (optional)
1. **[Step 1 headline]**: [One sentence]
2. **[Step 2 headline]**: [One sentence]
3. **[Step 3 headline]**: [One sentence]

---

## Objection handling (optional)
**[Common concern]**: [Direct response]
**[Common concern]**: [Direct response]

---

## Secondary CTA section

### Headline
[Reinforcement of key benefit or urgency]

### CTA
**Button text**: [Same or variant of primary CTA]
**Supporting text**: [Address final hesitation]

---

## Footer elements
- Trust badges (security, certifications)
- Links (Privacy, Terms, Contact)
```

## CTA button templates

```md
## Primary CTA patterns

| Pattern | Example | Best for |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Action + benefit | "Start saving time" | Clear value |
| Get + outcome | "Get your free report" | Lead gen |
| Action + specificity | "Start 14-day trial" | Trials |
| Simple action | "Sign up free" | Low friction |

## Supporting text patterns

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Friction reducer | "No credit card required" |
| Time frame | "Set up in 5 minutes" |
| Guarantee | "Cancel anytime" |
| Social proof | "Join 10,000+ teams" |
```

## Headline formulas

```md
## Headline patterns

### End benefit headline
[What they get] — [In how long/how easily]
Example: "Beautiful proposals in 5 minutes"

### Pain-point headline
Stop [pain]. Start [gain].
Example: "Stop chasing invoices. Start getting paid."

### Specific outcome headline
[Number] [audience] [achieve outcome] [with/by doing]
Example: "50,000 teams ship faster with FeatureDock"

### Question headline
[Question that answer is your product]
Example: "What if your database optimized itself?"
```

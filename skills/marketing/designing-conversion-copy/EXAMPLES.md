## Example 4: Signup form optimization

**Input:**
- Product: Newsletter
- Current form: Name, Email, Company, Role, Phone
- Conversion goal: Increase signups

**Output:**
```md
## Before (friction-heavy)
- Name (required)
- Email (required)
- Company (required)
- Role (required)
- Phone (required)

[Subscribe]

---

## After (friction-reduced)

### Get weekly insights on [topic]

[Email address field]

[Send me the newsletter]

üîí No spam. Unsubscribe anytime.

*Join 15,000 [professionals] who read this every week.*

---

**Rationale:**
- Removed 4 unnecessary fields (Name, Company, Role, Phone can be collected later)
- Added value-focused headline
- Changed CTA from generic "Subscribe" to specific "Send me the newsletter"
- Added trust signal (privacy note)
- Added social proof (subscriber count)
```

## Example 5: Edge case ‚Äî High-consideration B2B

**Input:**
- Product: Enterprise security software ($50K+ ACV)
- Conversion goal: Request demo (not signup)
- Audience: CISOs (very skeptical, short on time)
- Traffic: Direct/branded search

**Output:**
```md
## See how [Company] protects [use case] at scale

Request a technical demo tailored to your environment. We'll show you:
- How we integrate with your existing stack
- Detection capabilities for [specific threat type]
- Deployment timeline and resource requirements

[Request demo]

**What to expect:**
30-minute technical walkthrough with a solutions engineer. No sales pitch‚Äîjust your questions answered.

*Trusted by 3 of the Fortune 10 and 200+ enterprises worldwide.*

---

**Note:** For high-consideration B2B:
- Lead with specificity ("tailored to your environment")
- Set expectations about the experience ("30-minute", "solutions engineer")
- Defuse sales resistance ("No sales pitch")
- Social proof emphasizes scale and credibility
```

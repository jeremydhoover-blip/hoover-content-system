# Templates

## Landing page hero section

```md
## [Headline: Outcome or transformation, not feature]

[Subhead: Supporting detail that adds specificity or addresses "how"]

[Social proof micro-element: "Trusted by 10,000+ teams" or star rating]

[Primary CTA button: Action verb + value]

[Secondary CTA link: Lower-commitment alternative]

[Visual: Product screenshot, demo, or relevant image]
```

## Landing page full structure

```md
# [Page Title / H1: SEO + Value proposition]

## Hero Section
[Headline]
[Subhead]
[Primary CTA] [Secondary link]

## Problem / Pain Section
[2-3 sentences or bullets describing the pain your audience feels]

## Solution Section
### [Benefit 1 headline]
[Supporting copy: 1-2 sentences + proof]

### [Benefit 2 headline]
[Supporting copy]

### [Benefit 3 headline]
[Supporting copy]

## Social Proof Section
[Logos or testimonial quotes]
[Specific metrics or results]

## How It Works (optional)
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

## Objection Handling / FAQ
**[Objection as question]**
[Answer that resolves concern]

## Final CTA Section
[Reinforcing headline]
[CTA button]
[Objection defeater: "No credit card required" or "Cancel anytime"]
```

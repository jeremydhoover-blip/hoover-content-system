**Input:**
- Product: Analytics dashboard (existing, 3 years old)
- Situation: Previously positioned as "powerful analytics," now repositioning for ease of use
- Challenge: Can't contradict what existing customers believe; need graceful evolution
- Audience: Data teams (existing), business users (new target)

**Output:**

```md

**New:** Analytics that make sense.

### Existing customers (data teams)

### New target (business users)
**Lead message:** Analytics you can actually use
**Supporting:** "Stop waiting for the data teamâ€”get answers yourself"

## Transition communication

### What's new

- Pre-built dashboards
- Simplified sharing permissions
```

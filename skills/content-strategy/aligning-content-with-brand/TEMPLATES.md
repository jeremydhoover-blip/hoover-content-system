```md
## Brand alignment scoring

| Score | Definition | Action |
|-------|------------|--------|
| 5 | Exemplary—could be used as brand example | Publish; add to example library |
| 4 | Strong—clearly on-brand | Publish |
| 3 | Acceptable—no violations, but not distinctive | Publish; note for improvement |
| 2 | Weak—minor misalignment | Revise before publishing |
| 1 | Off-brand—contradicts brand attributes | Do not publish; significant revision |
```

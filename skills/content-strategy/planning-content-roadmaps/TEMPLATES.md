## Prioritization matrix

```md
# Content Prioritization Matrix

## Scoring criteria

### Impact (1-5)
- 5: Directly drives key business metric
- 4: High user need, strong engagement expected
- 3: Moderate user need or business value
- 2: Nice to have, incremental improvement
- 1: Low impact, minimal user demand

### Effort (1-5)
- 5: Multi-week project, complex dependencies
- 4: 1-2 weeks, some dependencies
- 3: Several days, straightforward
- 2: Few hours to a day
- 1: Quick win, minimal effort

## Prioritized backlog

| Item | Impact | Effort | Score | Priority |
|------|--------|--------|-------|----------|
| [Item 1] | 5 | 2 | 2.5 | P1 |
| [Item 2] | 4 | 3 | 1.3 | P2 |
| [Item 3] | 3 | 4 | 0.75 | P3 |

*Score = Impact / Effort. Higher = prioritize.*
```

# Templates

## Standard blog post structure

```md
# [Headline: Benefit or curiosity-driven]

*[Reading time] · [Category or topic tag]*

[Introduction: 2-3 paragraphs]
- Paragraph 1: Hook (question, bold claim, or story)
- Paragraph 2: Context (why this matters now)
- Paragraph 3: Promise (what reader will learn/gain)

---

## [Section 1: H2 header]

[2-4 paragraphs of content]

[Example, quote, or visual if relevant]

## [Section 2: H2 header]

[2-4 paragraphs of content]

### [Subsection: H3 if needed]

[Supporting detail]

## [Section 3: H2 header]

[2-4 paragraphs of content]

---

## Key takeaway / Conclusion

[1-2 paragraph summary of main point]

[CTA: What to do next—try product, read related post, comment]

---

*[Author bio or related posts section]*
```

## Thought leadership post

```md
# [Provocative or contrarian headline]

[Opening: Personal observation or industry trend that prompted this]

## The conventional wisdom

[What most people believe or do]

## Why that's wrong (or incomplete)

[Your counterargument with evidence]

## A better approach

[Your recommended framework or mindset]

## What this looks like in practice

[Concrete example or case]

## The bottom line

[Restate thesis + implication for reader]

---

*[About the author]*
```

## Tutorial / How-to post

```md
# How to [achieve specific outcome]

*[Time estimate] · [Difficulty: Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced]*

[Introduction: What you'll accomplish and why it matters]

## Prerequisites
- [Tool or knowledge 1]
- [Tool or knowledge 2]

## Step 1: [Action verb + object]

[Explanation + why this step matters]

```code or example```

## Step 2: [Action verb + object]

[Explanation]

```code or example```

## Step 3: [Action verb + object]

[Explanation]

## Troubleshooting / Common issues

**[Issue 1]**: [Solution]
**[Issue 2]**: [Solution]

## Wrap-up

[Summary of what was accomplished]

[Next steps or related tutorials]
```

## Listicle

```md
# [Number] [Category] [for outcome/audience]

[Introduction: Why these matter, how they were selected]

## 1. [Item name]

[2-3 sentences: What it is and why it's on the list]

**Best for**: [Use case]

## 2. [Item name]

[2-3 sentences]

**Best for**: [Use case]

[Continue pattern...]

## Which one is right for you?

[Brief decision guide or summary table]

---

[CTA]
```

## Announcement post

```md
# Introducing [Feature/Product]: [Benefit statement]

[Opening: What we're launching and why]

## What it does

[Clear explanation of capability]

[Screenshot or visual]

## Why we built it

[Customer problem or feedback that drove this]

## How to get started

[Steps to access or try]

## What's next

[Roadmap teaser if relevant]

---

[CTA: Try it / Learn more / Give feedback]
```

## Headline variants template

```md
## Headline Options

**Primary**: [Working title]

**Alternatives**:
1. [How-to variant]: How to [achieve X] [in timeframe/without pain]
2. [Number variant]: [N] Ways to [achieve X]
3. [Question variant]: [Question your audience is asking]?
4. [Contrarian variant]: Why [common belief] is wrong
5. [Benefit variant]: [Achieve X] [qualifier: faster, easier, better]
```

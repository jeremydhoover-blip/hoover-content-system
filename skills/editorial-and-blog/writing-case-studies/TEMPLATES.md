# Templates

## Standard case study (full format)

```md
# [Customer Name]: [Result or Transformation Statement]

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Company** | [Customer name] |
| **Industry** | [Industry] |
| **Size** | [Employees or revenue range] |
| **Use case** | [Primary use case] |

### Key Results
- **[Metric 1]**: [Improvement—e.g., "40% faster processing"]
- **[Metric 2]**: [Improvement]
- **[Metric 3]**: [Improvement]

---

## The Challenge

[2-3 paragraphs describing the situation before your solution]

- What problem were they facing?
- What was the business impact of that problem?
- What had they tried before?

> "[Quote from customer describing the pain point]"
> — [Name, Title, Company]

---

## The Solution

[2-3 paragraphs describing how they implemented your solution]

- What specifically did they deploy?
- How did implementation go?
- What was the timeline?

### Implementation Highlights
- [Key aspect of implementation]
- [Key aspect of implementation]
- [Integration or customization if relevant]

---

## The Results

[2-3 paragraphs on outcomes and impact]

### Quantified Impact

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| [Metric 1] | [Value] | [Value] | [% or absolute] |
| [Metric 2] | [Value] | [Value] | [% or absolute] |
| [Metric 3] | [Value] | [Value] | [% or absolute] |

> "[Quote from customer about results or experience]"
> — [Name, Title, Company]

---

## What's Next

[1 paragraph on future plans or expansion]

---

## About [Customer Name]

[1-2 sentence company description]

[Company website URL]

---

*Ready to achieve similar results? [CTA: Contact us / Start trial / Request demo]*
```

## One-page case study (compact)

```md
# [Customer Name] achieves [key result] with [Your Product]

**Industry**: [Industry] | **Size**: [Size] | **Use case**: [Use case]

## Challenge
[2-3 sentences on the problem]

## Solution
[2-3 sentences on what was implemented]

## Results
- **[X%]** [improvement in metric]
- **[Y hours]** [time saved per week/month]
- **[Z]** [other quantified result]

> "[Short customer quote—2-3 sentences max]"
> — [Name, Title]

[CTA: Read full story → / Contact us →]
```

## Video case study script outline

```md
# [Customer Name] Case Study — Video Script

## Opening (0:00-0:30)
[Customer introduces themselves and company]

"Hi, I'm [Name], [Title] at [Company]. We [brief company description]."

## The Problem (0:30-1:30)
[Customer describes challenge in their own words]

Key points to cover:
- What wasn't working
- Impact on their team/business
- What they tried before

## Finding the Solution (1:30-2:30)
[How they discovered and evaluated your product]

- What drew them to your solution
- Decision criteria
- Implementation experience

## The Results (2:30-3:30)
[Specific outcomes and metrics]

- Before/after comparison
- Quantified improvements
- Team reaction

## Looking Forward (3:30-4:00)
[Future plans and recommendation]

"I'd recommend [Product] to anyone who [target audience situation]."

## B-roll suggestions
- [Customer using product]
- [Team collaboration]
- [Dashboard/results view]
```

# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Provide constructive feedback focused on the work, not the person
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them
- Prioritize the community's well-being over individual preferences

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language
- Personal attacks or inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code applies to all spaces associated with this project, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code, and to ban contributors for repeated or severe violations.
